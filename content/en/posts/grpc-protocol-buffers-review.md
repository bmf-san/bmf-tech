# Links
## gRPC
- [grpc.io](https://grpc.io/)
  - Official documentation
- [zenn.dev - Learn by Doing! First gRPC](https://zenn.dev/hsaki/books/golang-grpc-starting)

- [www.wantedly.com - gRPC Internal - The World Revealed by gRPC Design and Internal Implementation](https://www.wantedly.com/companies/wantedly/post_articles/219429)

  - Explanation of load balancing for gRPC servers
- [deeeet.com - Running gRPC Services on Kubernetes](https://deeeet.com/writing/2018/03/30/kubernetes-grpc/)
