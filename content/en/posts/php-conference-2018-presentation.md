# PHP Conference 2018
I gave a lightning talk on the theme "Engineer Career Perspectives from Those in Their 20s."

I was glad to be able to present again in the LT slot this year, following last year's PHP Conference 2017, but I hope to be selected for a 25-minute slot next year.

This is my third time attending the PHP Conference, and I feel the warmth of the PHP community every time.

Since I hadn't presented at all since last year's PHP Conference, this event has become a sort of culmination or starting point for me in the year.

With each attendance, I sometimes feel a sense of personal growth. (I’ve started to understand things that I couldn’t relate to or comprehend before...)

This year, the theme of the conference was Growth, which seems to reflect my past year.

After reflecting on last year, I focused on input this year, and I feel like it has become a year that connects to next year's theme of beyond.

Next year, while it won't be driven by presentations, I want to increase opportunities to present on technical themes, share what I've learned, and adopt an approach that somewhat forces me to produce outputs.

From Growth to beyond, I aim to present in a 25-minute slot next year.
