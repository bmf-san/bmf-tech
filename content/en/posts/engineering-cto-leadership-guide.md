[Guide for Engineering Executives: Technical Leadership to Drive Organizational Success](https://amzn.to/46kGE0z) is a book that discusses the mindset and practices necessary for leading a technical organization as a VP of Engineering or CTO. It specifically addresses the extensive responsibilities of a technical leader, including hiring, organizational design, technical strategy, and collaboration with management. Rather than focusing on individual management techniques, it emphasizes a holistic view of the organization as a system, making it a compass for engineers and EMs aspiring to become Engineering Executives in the future, as well as current VPEs.
