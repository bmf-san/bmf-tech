[The Art of Leadership: Paying Attention to the Small Things](https://amzn.to/4aEtHjr) is a book I recently read. 

This book discusses the idea that the small, everyday actions of an engineering leader play a crucial role in shaping organizational culture and building trust within the team. It emphasizes the importance of seemingly minor aspects, such as how you speak during meetings, deliver feedback, or conduct 1-on-1s, illustrating how these actions, though subtle, can lead to significant differences over time. 

The book focuses on interpersonal skills and habits that are often overlooked as technically skilled engineers transition into leadership roles. It serves as a great opportunity to reflect on and improve your daily actions.
