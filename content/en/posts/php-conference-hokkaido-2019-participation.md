# Attended PHP Conference Hokkaido 2019
I participated in the PHP conference after two months. (Last time I attended and spoke at [PHP Conference Fukuoka 2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHPカンファレンス福岡2019に参加・登壇してきました))

Hokkaido is a place I have a connection with, having lived there for a few years during my childhood and with my grandparents residing there. I felt a different emotion compared to other conferences, spending time there with a poetic and emotional feeling. (I visited my old house and the park I used to play in.)
