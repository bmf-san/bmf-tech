[The Secrets of Unicorn Companies - Software Development and Work Styles Learned from Spotify](https://amzn.to/44PiyJp) was read.

You can learn about the organizational theory and culture of unicorn companies.

As mentioned in the afterword by the author, unicorn companies do not practice Scrum. I believe how one perceives this can change the overall impression of the book.

Personally, I see it as reaching beyond Scrum, which has achieved the stage of Shu-Ha-Ri. The main theme (secret) of this book, I think, is what kind of culture and qualities support growth, regardless of methodology.
