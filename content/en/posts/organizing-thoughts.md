[I read the Wide Edition of The Art of Thought Organization](https://amzn.to/3D4s3KR).

The first edition was published just before the internet became widespread, yet its perspective on computers still resonates today, which I found intriguing.

This book emphasizes the importance of thinking for oneself and not relying solely on knowledge.

Although written in an essay style rather than a business-like tone, it conveys ideas for organizing thoughts.
