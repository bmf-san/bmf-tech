[Wide Edition: The Art of Organizing Thoughts](https://amzn.to/3D4s3KR) was read.

The first edition was published just before the internet became widespread, and the way of thinking about computers resonates with modern times, which I found intriguing.

This book conveys the importance of thinking for oneself and maintaining an attitude that does not rely on knowledge.

Although it is not written in a business-like essay style, it discusses the philosophy for organizing thoughts.
