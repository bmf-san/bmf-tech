[The Go Programming Language, 2nd Edition - An Idiomatic Go Practical Guide for Programmers from Other Languages](https://amzn.to/3J2NzmR) is a book I read.

I had the opportunity to write Go at work after a long time, so I read it to catch up on new information and review.

This book is not just an introductory text; it introduces many of Go's philosophies and idioms, deepening the understanding of the language.

It covers topics that other introductory books might overlook, making it a valuable resource.
