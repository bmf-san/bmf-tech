I read the [The Go Programming Language, 2nd Edition: An Idiomatic Go Practical Guide for Programmers from Other Languages](https://amzn.to/3J2NzmR).

Having recently had the opportunity to write Go at work after a long time, I read this book to catch up on new information and review what I already knew.

This book is not just an introductory text; it introduces many of Go's philosophies and idioms, allowing for a deeper understanding of the language.

It covers topics that other introductory books might overlook, making it a valuable resource.
