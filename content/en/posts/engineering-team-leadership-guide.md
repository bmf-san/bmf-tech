[Engineering Team Leadership: A Practical Guide for Individual Contributors and Managers Inspired by Google](https://amzn.to/4kMMC0j) is a book I recently read. This book draws inspiration from Google's engineering culture and discusses how to effectively lead engineering teams from the perspectives of both ICs and managers. 

It systematically covers themes rooted in daily work, such as career ladders, code reviews, handling technical debt, and project management. This practical guidebook is aimed at all engineers, from senior engineers to engineering managers, who want to contribute to their teams and influence those around them.
