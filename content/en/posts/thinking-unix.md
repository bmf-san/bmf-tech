[The UNIX Philosophy](https://amzn.to/4adUXDh) is a book that summarizes the thoughts and philosophy of UNIX in a concise 150 pages.

This book also teaches a way of thinking that is applicable to coding.

It contains ideas that can be universally applied when considering the design of something, making it a valuable reference when tackling design challenges.
