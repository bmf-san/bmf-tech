# Overview
A compilation of books and links I referred to while studying golang.

# Books
- [プログラミング言語Go](https://amzn.to/3UDJQPp)
    - The Bible
- [スターティングGo言語](https://amzn.to/3Wmulge)
    - Easy to read and understand. Might be too simple for those with a strong programming background.
- [Go言語によるWebアプリケーション開発](https://amzn.to/3WjXQis)
- [Goプログラミング実践入門 標準ライブラリでゼロからWebアプリを作る](https://amzn.to/4aWBGHs)

# Links
- [golang.jp](http://golang.jp/)
- [A Tour of Go](https://go-tour-jp.appspot.com/list)
- [astaxie/build-web-application-with-golang](https://github.com/astaxie/build-web-application-with-golang/blob/master/ja/preface.md)
    - You can get a good grasp of the content without purchasing the book by reading this.
- [Go Data Structures](https://research.swtch.com/godata)
    - Detailed information on Go's data structures.
