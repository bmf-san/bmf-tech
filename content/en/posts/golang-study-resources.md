# Overview
A summary of books and links that were referenced while studying Golang.

# Books
- [The Go Programming Language](https://amzn.to/3UDJQPp)
    - The definitive guide
- [Starting Go Language](https://amzn.to/3Wmulge)
    - Easy to read. Clear. The content may be too simple for those with a high level of programming literacy.
- [Web Application Development with Go](https://amzn.to/3WjXQis)
- [Practical Introduction to Go Programming: Building Web Applications from Scratch with the Standard Library](https://amzn.to/4aWBGHs)

# Links
- [golang.jp](http://golang.jp/)
- [A Tour of Go](https://go-tour-jp.appspot.com/list)
- [astaxie/build-web-application-with-golang](https://github.com/astaxie/build-web-application-with-golang/blob/master/ja/preface.md)
    - I feel that reading this will allow you to catch up on the general concepts without purchasing the book.
- [Go Data Structures](https://research.swtch.com/godata)
    - Detailed information about Go's data structures.
