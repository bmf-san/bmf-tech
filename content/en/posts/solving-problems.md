[How to Solve Problems](https://amzn.to/4fh4mNS) is a book I read.

Although it's quite an old book, it discusses methods of thinking applicable to problems based on mathematics.

Since it's not a recent book, it can be a bit difficult to read.

I am thoroughly reminded that to solve problems, it is essential to first understand the problem well and to approach it from various angles through trial and error.
