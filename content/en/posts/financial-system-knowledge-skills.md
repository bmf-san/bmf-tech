[Knowledge and Skills of Financial Systems for Engineers](https://amzn.to/3wd9dhG) was read.

It gives more of an impression of domain knowledge in finance rather than systematic discussions. It explains the operations and systems of banks, insurance, securities, and credit card companies. I thought it would be a useful book even for those involved in payment systems.
