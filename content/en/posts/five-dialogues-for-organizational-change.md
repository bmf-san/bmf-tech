[Five Dialogues to Transform Organizations - Creating an Agile Organizational Culture Through Dialogue](https://amzn.to/4aRIaJc) is a book that discusses the five types of dialogues necessary to cultivate an agile organizational culture. It presents dialogue formats based on themes such as goals, constraints, progress, processes, and learning, providing concrete explanations for dialogue practices that enable teams and organizations to operate autonomously. Rather than top-down transformation, it aims for cultural change initiated through dialogue, making it suitable for managers and Scrum Masters who want to promote Agile implementation, as well as those engaged in organizational development.
