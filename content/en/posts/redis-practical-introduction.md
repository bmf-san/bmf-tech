[Practical Introduction to Redis: From Technical Mechanisms to Real-World Applications](https://amzn.to/412eRyA) is a book I read.

As the title suggests, it contains practical content, but it also covers a lot about Redis that makes you want to add a detailed explanation to the title.

There are surprisingly few books written in Japanese about Redis, and I think this book is a valuable addition.

I particularly appreciated the thorough coverage of operational management. Although my understanding is shallow, I also liked that it discusses internal implementations.

I want to keep this book on hand so I can refer to it whenever I have questions about Redis.
