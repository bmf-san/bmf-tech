# Overview
I participated in all official PHP conferences held in Japan in 2019, so I will summarize my participation records.

# Summary of PHP Conference Participation Reports
Starting in Sendai and ending in Tokyo. All conferences outside of Tokyo were my first time attending.

- [Participated in PHP Conference Sendai 2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHP%E3%82%AB%E3%83%B3%E3%83%95%E3%82%A1%E3%83%AC%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B9%E4%BB%99%E5%8F%B02019%E3%81%AB%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F)
- [Laravel JP Conference](https://conference2019.laravel.jp/)
The first Laravel conference held in Japan. I participated as both staff and speaker. I plan to participate as staff again next year.
cf. [Laravel JP Conference 2020](https://conference2020.laravel.jp/)
- [Participated and spoke at PHPerKaigi2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHPerKaigi2019%E3%81%AB%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E3%83%BB%E7%99%BB%E5%A3%87%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E3%81%8D%E3%81%BE%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F)
- [Participated and spoke at PHP Conference Fukuoka 2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHP%E3%82%AB%E3%83%B3%E3%83%95%E3%82%A1%E3%83%AC%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B9%E7%A6%8F%E5%B2%A12019%E3%81%AB%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E3%83%BB%E7%99%BB%E5%A3%87%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E3%81%8D%E3%81%BE%E3%81%97%E3%81%9F)
I had submitted a CFP for the past two years but was continuously rejected in Fukuoka. I had heard good feedback from participants every year, and I was determined to participate through a CFP this year, so I was glad to be able to attend and speak successfully.
- [Participated in PHP Conference Hokkaido 2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHP%E3%82%AB%E3%83%B3%E3%83%95%E3%82%A1%E3%83%AC%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B9%E5%8C%97%E6%B5%B7%E9%81%932019%E3%81%AB%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E3%81%8D%E3%81%9F)
- [Participated in PHP Conference Okinawa 2019](https://bmf-tech.com/posts/PHP%E3%82%AB%E3%83%B3%E3%83%95%E3%82%A1%E3%83%AC%E3%83%B3%E3%82%B9%E6%B2%96%E7%B8%842019%E3%81%AB%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E3%81%97%E3%81%A6%E3%81%8D%E3%81%9F)
This was the first time held. Since it was in Okinawa, it seemed like there was a high barrier for participants from outside the prefecture, but there were quite a few participants from outside, and I think it was a great success. There were also participants from Taiwan, adding an international flavor.
- [CakeFest](https://cakefest.org/)
The first CakeFest conference held in Japan. As an international conference, it had a strong global feel, making it feel like participating in an overseas conference while being in Japan. The sessions covered themes related to PHP web development in general, not just CakePHP, making it an interesting conference even for those who do not usually use CakePHP.
- [PHP CONFERENCE JAPAN 2019](https://phpcon.php.gr.jp/2019/)
This year's PHP Conference Tokyo had a special feel as it marked the 20th anniversary. I have participated for three consecutive years, but this year was particularly impressive as [Sebastian Bergmann](https://twitter.com/s_bergmann), the creator of PHPUnit, was speaking.
- I couldn't fully participate in [Laravel JP Conference](https://conference2019.laravel.jp/), [CakeFest](https://cakefest.org/), and [PHP CONFERENCE JAPAN 2019](https://phpcon.php.gr.jp/2019/), so I slacked off on the reports..

Looking back at the sessions of each conference, I think there were relatively many sessions on application design and testing this year.

# Thoughts
I was able to feel firsthand that there are people across the country who are passionate about PHP, sharing similar interests and facing similar problems, and I felt like I experienced the connections within the domestic PHP community. I am deeply grateful to my company for supporting my participation. Next year, I hope to attend an overseas conference...
