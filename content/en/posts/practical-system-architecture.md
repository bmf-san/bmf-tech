[Practical Methods for Building System Architecture](https://amzn.to/46f3iID) was read.

A practical guide for engineers working on architecture design in the field.

While the previous book, [Principles of Building System Architecture: The Three Thoughts IT Architects Should Have](https://amzn.to/4lDpXCy), focused on "why," this book explains "how."

It consistently covers everything from requirements definition to logical design and physical design, introducing a wealth of specific methods useful in the field, such as documentation through views, leveraging reusable assets, and ways to record decisions.

The book also touches on the role of architects and the necessary skills, making it a valuable read for engineers who want to systematically enhance their design capabilities.
