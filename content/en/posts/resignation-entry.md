I have resigned from my current job after 5 years and 5 months.

This was the company where I worked the longest in my career.

Before joining, I only had experience as an application engineer, and I believe that the experiences and challenges I faced at this company greatly supported my growth.

Experiencing the company's IPO phase and witnessing the growth of the organization and business up close was an invaluable experience.

In my personal life, I started living alone for the first time, met my wife, cohabited, got married, had children, and took parental leave, going through various life stages. The person I was in my 20s has now turned into someone in their 30s, having aged five years without realizing it.

The company also supported me through these personal changes.

I am truly grateful to the company that has significantly supported both my work and personal life.

I was very fortunate to work in a fun environment and to have respected bosses and colleagues.

As I considered resigning, I found it difficult to leave because there were so many wonderful people.

I would be happy if we could continue our relationships and have opportunities to work together again in the future.

This company holds a special place in my life, and I still love its business and products, but I decided to resign because I found a new challenge.

Thank you very much for all the support over the past five and a half years.
