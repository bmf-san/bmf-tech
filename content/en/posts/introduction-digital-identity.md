[Introduction to Digital Identity - Essential Authentication, Authorization, and ID Management for Web Services](https://amzn.to/4b2GqOg) is a book that clearly explains the basics of authentication, authorization, and ID management in web services. It discusses the fundamental mechanisms and use cases of OAuth 2.0, OpenID Connect, and SAML, along with practical examples, covering a wide range of topics from single sign-on and social login to the utilization of IDaaS. This book is ideal for developers looking to systematically organize their knowledge around authentication and for engineers aiming to implement ID management systems in their products.
