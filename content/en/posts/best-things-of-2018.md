As 2018 is coming to an end, I want to list the things I bought this year that I liked.

This year was more fulfilling than last year, and I bought a variety of items.

Since ranking them is a hassle, I will just list them casually.

# Long Wallet
I bought a nice long wallet made of horse leather. (To be precise, it was a gift!)

The outside is made of horse leather and the inside is made of vegetable-tanned leather, which develops a nice patina the more you use it.

I've only been using it for less than a year, but I really like how the leather feels.

[https://www.noi-japan.com/noijapan/7.1/105625/:title]

# Coin Purse
This is from the same brand as the long wallet, and it's a proper leather product, making it easy to use.

I usually don't put coins in the long wallet, so I have a separate coin purse, which I also like.

[https://www.noi-japan.com/noijapan/7.1/106274/:embed:cite]

# iPad
I bought it mainly to read e-books, and it's really good.

Some people might find it a bit heavy, but since I work out regularly, it's not much of a burden for me.

The size is also just right for reading.

I actually regret not buying it sooner.

[https://www.apple.com/jp/shop/buy-ipad/ipad-9-7/32gb-%E3%82%B9%E3%83%9A%E3%83%BC%E3%82%B9%E3%82%B0%E3%83%AC%E3%82%A4-wifi:embed:cite]

# Cross Bike
Fast. Comfortable. Fun.

I can’t go back to a mama-chari.

When I lived at my parents' house, it took about 10 minutes to get to the station on a mama-chari, but with the cross bike, it was shortened to about 5 minutes.

I wanted to minimize travel time, but now I live just a 2-3 minute walk from the station, so I haven't had many opportunities to ride it lately...

However, it's fun to ride, so I go cycling when I have time.

[http://www.japan.bianchi.com/category.cgi?mode=category_detail&bik_Code_prm=JP19BS67084332&big_code=03&mdl_code=02:embed:cite]
(I'm pretty sure I bought the 2018 model)

# WiFi
A Chinese WiFi speaker.

The sound quality is decent, but it has good portability and seems quite durable, making it a good value.

I feel that recent Chinese audio equipment has good quality, so I trust it.

[https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B0787KGCM1/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o01_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1:title]

# Rental
It's a bit different to say I bought this, but I moved out from my parents' house to a certain area in Tokyo.

My commute is now under 20 minutes door-to-door, so my travel time has significantly decreased.

I wanted to make more time for myself after changing jobs, so even though it costs a bit more, I think it was a good investment overall.

# Desk Stand
I've been using a typical study desk at home for a long time, but adding a desk stand has made my workspace much tidier, so I recommend it.

[https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B0142Q0Z52/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o08_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1]

# Sofa
I bought a sofa from a brand I didn’t know well when I started living alone.

It's worth the price, and for the quality at this price point, I’m quite satisfied.

[https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B07C15RCLR/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o06_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1]

# Cote&Ciel Shoulder Bag
Since the backpack I bought previously was great, I also purchased a shoulder bag.

It seems sturdy, so I feel secure carrying my Mac and iPad.

I recommend looking for reasonably priced used items on Rakuten.

# tipx
It's more of a membership than a purchase, but...

I changed gyms after moving.

When I was looking for a gym around Shibuya Station, tipx matched my requirements.

It feels a bit pricey, but I’m satisfied as it helps me maintain my motivation to work out.

# Air Purifier
I bought this because my room gets dry and the air tends to stagnate.

I think it's common to find air purifiers in hotels, and experiencing it made me feel that the air is quite nice (though it might just be my imagination...), so I ended up buying this appliance.

It has a humidifying function, so it prevents dryness too!

I feel like it clears up odors and dust in the room as well.

[https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B01M09SIHT/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o05_?ie=UTF8&psc=1]

# Display
I had been using an old Apple Cinema Display, but I decided to replace it when I moved.

It's cost-effective, so I have no complaints.

[https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B077SBB5SH/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o04_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1:title]

# Nintendo Switch
I impulsively bought it because I wanted to play Super Smash Bros. (I also bought Splatoon 2).

It's been about 10 years since I bought a game console, and the recent games are amazing. Nintendo is incredible. The UI/UX is wonderful.

Usually, I either write code or read books, but I think it will help relieve stress, so I want to be careful not to overdo it.

Very convenient.

I use it attached to the front door.

# Thoughts
2018 was a year where I bought quite a lot more than usual.

I had a few more furniture items that I could have introduced, but I decided against it as it would be endless.

By the way, I made one more expensive purchase, but I’ll save that for another time.

Having gathered a variety of items, I feel like I won’t be buying much next year, but who knows...
