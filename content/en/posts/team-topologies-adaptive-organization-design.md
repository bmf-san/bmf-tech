[Team Topologies: Adaptive Organization Design for Rapid Delivery of Valuable Software](https://amzn.to/4ilaaI2) is a book that presents a team-first approach to organizational design and architecture.

It illustrates patterns of team design within organizations, but I understood that the key point is how to perceive team communication and interactions between teams.

Since the optimal structure varies by organization, I read the patterns presented here as just examples.

Recently, I feel like I often see organizational structures similar to these patterns, so personally, I didn't find it particularly novel. (I can't speak for the time when it was released...)
