[Decision-Making Techniques for Software Architects: Utilizing Leadership, Technology, and Product Management](https://amzn.to/3DysQ7d) is a book I read.

This book is one I participated in the translation review for, and I revisited it after receiving a complimentary copy.

The focus on the decision-making of architects is groundbreaking and provides valuable insights for those pursuing architectural design.

The book introduces five questions and seven principles that serve as tools to confront the uncertainties of architecture.

In particular, the five questions are critical inquiries in architectural decision-making that I want to keep in mind at all times.
