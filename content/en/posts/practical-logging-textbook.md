This book systematically explains the design, collection, analysis, and utilization of system logs from the basics to practical applications.  
It covers a wide range of essential knowledge for practical work, including types of logs, methods of structuring them, building collection infrastructure, and utilizing them for troubleshooting and performance optimization. Additionally, it delves into the application of log analysis using machine learning, making it a practical book that engineers who deal with logs daily, such as those in infrastructure, backend, and SRE, would want to keep on hand.
