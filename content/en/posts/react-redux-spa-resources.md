This is a rough note.
Here are the repositories and articles I referred to when trying to build an SPA with React and Redux.

There was a decent amount of information in Japanese to understand the concept of the Redux architecture. However, it took some effort to find sources that seemed relevant to what I wanted to create. Differences in JS specifications, build environments like compilers and task runners, version differences in React itself, and related package versions made it quite challenging for someone like me who was focused solely on PHP...

After doing a lot of research, I finally got to the point where I could start coding, so I will list the repositories and articles that were helpful at that time.

*I will update this as needed.*
