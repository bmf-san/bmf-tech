# Overview
I had the opportunity to present using StreamYard, so I made a note of the process.

This guide summarizes the steps to display the Keynote presenter view while sharing only the current slide in StreamYard.

# Requirements
You need two displays.

# Steps
1. Open your presentation in Keynote. Click **Play in Window**.

![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](/assets/images/posts/streamyard-keynote-presenter-display/140072377-36300e50-c3ad-43b6-83fc-0415d308935e.png)

2. In the top right of the playing window, click **Show Presenter Display in Window**.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](/assets/images/posts/streamyard-keynote-presenter-display/140072398-3222a3ce-ee02-40f5-9b88-c6082a2c8770.png)

3. Confirm that the presenter display is visible.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](/assets/images/posts/streamyard-keynote-presenter-display/140072406-55241c14-3180-4f55-bcbe-1a995f79b936.png)

4. In StreamYard, go to Share > Share Screen > Window, and share the playing slideshow.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](/assets/images/posts/streamyard-keynote-presenter-display/140072410-b54b5936-1357-41ca-b559-cfec3a9fd832.png)

You can share the playing slideshow on one display while viewing the presenter display on the other.
