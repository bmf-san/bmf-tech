# Overview
I had the opportunity to present using StreamYard, so I’m jotting down how to do it.

This post summarizes the steps to display the Keynote presenter display while sharing only the currently playing slides on StreamYard.

# Requirements
You need two displays.

# Steps
1. Open your presentation materials in Keynote. Click **Play in Window**.

![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/13291041/140072377-36300e50-c3ad-43b6-83fc-0415d308935e.png)

2. In the upper right corner of the playing window, select **Show Presenter Display in Window**.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/13291041/140072398-3222a3ce-ee02-40f5-9b88-c6082a2c8770.png)

3. Confirm that the presenter display is shown.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/13291041/140072406-55241c14-3180-4f55-bcbe-1a995f79b936.png)

4. In StreamYard, go to Share > Share Screen > Window, and share the slideshow that is currently playing.
![Screenshot 2021-11-03 22 50 07](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/13291041/140072410-b54b5936-1357-41ca-b559-cfec3a9fd832.png)

You can share the currently playing slideshow on one display while viewing the presenter display on the other.
