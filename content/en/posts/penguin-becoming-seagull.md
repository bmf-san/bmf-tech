[The Penguin Who Became a Seagull](https://amzn.to/41zV76h) is a book I read.

It's not a story about a penguin becoming a seagull, but rather a fable about how to solve problems and form teams in environments that demand change.

The eight-step process for successful transformation is a concept that leaders and managers, or anyone in a position to bring about organizational change, should keep in mind.
