[The Penguin That Became a Seagull](https://amzn.to/41zV76h) is a story not about a penguin turning into a seagull, but rather a fable that teaches how to solve problems and form teams in an environment where change is required.

The eight-step process for successful transformation is a concept that leaders and managers, or anyone in a position to bring about change in an organization, should keep in mind.
