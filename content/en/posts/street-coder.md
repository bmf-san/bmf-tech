[Street Coder](https://amzn.to/4nF6SRG) is a book that shares tips from the field with a touch of humor.

It covers a wide range of topics from algorithms, testing, and refactoring to security and performance optimization, providing concrete guidance on how to tackle challenges encountered in the field.

For beginners, it offers a practical image of coding, while for experienced developers, it provides an opportunity to reflect on their skills and attitudes.
