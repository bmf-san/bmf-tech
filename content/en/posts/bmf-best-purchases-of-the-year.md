Let's look back at the things I bought in 2017 that I found worthwhile.

I picked out items that I personally feel have improved my quality of life.

# Bluetooth Headphones

[Mpow Bluetooth Earphones](https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B07289Q3F1/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o07_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1:title:title])

These are Bluetooth-compatible earphones.

They are reasonably priced.

There are wired and wireless versions of these earphones, but I bought the wired type because I think they are less likely to get lost.

They are very useful when running, at the gym, or on the train.

The only downside is that they run out of battery after about 4 hours even on a full charge, but the cost performance is good.

# Bluetooth 4.1 Transmitter Receiver

[TaoTronics Transmitter Receiver](https://www.amazon.co.jp/gp/product/B01EHMPUHO/ref=oh_aui_detailpage_o07_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1:title:title])

I purchased this to make my iPod Classic Bluetooth compatible.

It's also reasonably priced.

It can function as both a transmitter and a receiver, so the cost performance is good.

Recently, I've started listening to music on my iPhone, so I don't use it as much anymore.

# Cote&Ciel Bag

This is a sturdy and stylish bag.

I think the design can be used with any outfit.

It also goes well with coats.

# Megalos Kichijoji

I joined [Megalos Kichijoji](https://www.megalos.co.jp/kichijoji/).

It's a gym.

Going to the gym is an investment in health and achieving the ideal body.

Just by running and continuing strength training, a certain level of mental and physical health is guaranteed. (Probably)

I used to think, "It's a waste of time to exercise; I want to do things I enjoy," but not exercising is actually a waste.

I believe exercise is necessary to maintain and improve daily performance. (I have experienced this.)
