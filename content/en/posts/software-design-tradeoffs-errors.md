[Read Trade-offs and Mistakes in Software Design - Making Better Choices in Programming](https://amzn.to/4ausWrh).

Trade-offs are sometimes not documented. While the reasons for making a choice may be written down, the reasons for not choosing something are often not indicated.

This book emphasizes the importance of considering trade-offs in design decisions and provides examples of trade-offs in software design.

There were also chapters in the latter half that made me think about trade-offs.
