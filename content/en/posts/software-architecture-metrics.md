[Software Architecture Metrics - 10 Tips to Improve Architecture Quality](https://amzn.to/4jsa9T9) was read.

Multiple authors discuss various perspectives on metrics related to software. As indicated by the subtitle, each author's advice is presented, and while the chapters do not delve deeply into details, they serve as a good starting point to understand perspectives on architecture metrics.

Chapter 10, which introduces the GQM approach for defining metrics, left a strong impression on me personally.

Of course, the introduction of the fitness function presented in evolutionary architecture is noteworthy, and some metrics related to system quality are also introduced, which I found helpful.
